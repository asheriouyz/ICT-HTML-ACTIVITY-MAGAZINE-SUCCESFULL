Social Media and Teenagers

Welcome to our digital newspaper, where we explore the pressing issues shaping today’s youth. In this edition, we dive into the world of social media and its impact on teenagers. From mental health and self-esteem to online behavior and digital habits, we aim to shed light on both the positive and negative effects of social media in teen lives.

Inside, you will find:

  • Feature Articles analyzing trends, research, and real-life stories about social media usage among teens.
  • Opinion Pieces where experts and young voices share their insights and reflections.
    • Through these sections, our goal is to provide a comprehensive, informative, and relatable resource for teens, parents, and educators alike—a space to reflect, and learn in a rapidly evolving digital age
